Jordan Bell

Jordan Bell (born January 7, 1995) is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association. He played college basketball for the Oregon Ducks

Bell attended Long Beach Polytechnic High School in Long Beach, California.[1] He committed to the University of Oregon to play college basketball.

As a freshman at Oregon, Bell averaged 5.1 points, 6.1 rebounds and 2.7 blocks per game.[3] His 94 blocks were a school record for a season.[4] As a sophomore, he averaged 7.0 points, 5.4 rebounds and 1.8 blocks per game.[5] As a junior, Bell averaged 10.9 points, 8.8 rebounds and 2.3 blocks per game and helped lead Oregon to the Final Four.

On April 18, 2017, Bell declared for the 2017 NBA draft,[8] and was picked with the 38th pick by the Chicago Bulls and later traded to the Golden State Warriors.
